About Sleazy Al's





We here at Sleazy Al's Pizza Factory have been cooking homemade pizzas for generations, all with the help of our Great Great Great Grandma Al's "love". After all these years we have finally decided to enter the digital age and bring the sleazy, to you! Introducing our brand new online pizza creation tool, which allows you to digitally create a classic Sleazy Special right from the comfort of your own home, and get it delivered! We hope you enjoy this new age of Sleazy Al's Pizza.
